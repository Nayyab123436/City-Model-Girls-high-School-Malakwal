About the school

A school built on trust, quietly proven over the years

City Model Girls High School, Malakwal, has grown into a trusted name for girls' education in the region — combining a disciplined academic programme with a caring, close-knit campus environment.

Every subject we teach, from Mathematics to Tarjuma-tul-Quran, is taught with the same intention: to prepare confident, capable young women who carry their learning into their homes and communities with grace.
